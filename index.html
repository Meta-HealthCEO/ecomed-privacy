Eco-Flow Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Eco-Flow ("we", "our", "us") is a mobile application developed by Eco-Med (Pty) Ltd for managing homecare oxygen prescriptions and patient care. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register or log in, we collect: name, email address, phone number, and professional credentials (for healthcare providers).

Patient Data

Healthcare providers may access patient records including: names, ID numbers, contact details, medical aid information, diagnoses, and prescription details. This data is processed on behalf of the healthcare organization.

Device Information

We may collect device type, operating system, and app version for analytics and troubleshooting.

Camera & Photos

If you grant permission, we access your camera and photo library solely to capture and upload clinical documents.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Eco-Flow service
  • To authenticate your identity and manage access permissions
  • To process and track prescriptions
  • To communicate important updates about your account
  • To improve our application and services

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is transmitted using TLS encryption. Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using platform-native secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). Patient data is not stored locally on your device — it is fetched from our secure servers on demand.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Your healthcare organization (as the data controller)
  • Medical aid schemes (for authorization processing, as directed by your organization)
  • Law enforcement (if required by law)

6. POPIA Compliance

We comply with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa. You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Object to the processing of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator

7. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as your account is active and as required by healthcare regulations. Medical records are retained in accordance with the National Health Act and HPCSA guidelines.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for healthcare professionals and is not directed at children under 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or via email.
